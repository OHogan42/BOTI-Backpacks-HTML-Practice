About BOTI Backpacks

"Making quality backpacks since 2013."

Bigger on the Inside(BOTI) Backpacks was first conceived by Michael Hogan, David Phung, and some other scouts in a Communications merit badge class in 2013. It was just an idea then, but Michael founded the company in 2016 after discovering how to make an object bigger on the inside than the outside. He then made this scientifc discovery more cost effective. He also adjusted the weight, so the packs can be made extremely light by using an adjuster. What you are carrying makes no difference on the weight. We carry all of the following and more besides:

  1. School Backpacks
  2. Frame Packs
    • Internal
    • External
  3. Climbing Packs
  4. Mountaineering Packs
  5. Messenger Bags
  6. Laptop Bags
  7. Hunting Packs
  8. Daypacks
